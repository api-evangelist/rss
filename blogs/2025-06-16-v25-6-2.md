New Feature Enhanced Apple Podcasts Integration Our automatic Apple Podcasts submission tool has already helped thousands of podcasters get listed on Apple with one click. Now we’re making it even better: claiming your podcast on Apple Podcasts Connect is now more tightly integrated into the process, making it easier than ever to unlock audience insights and manage your show directly from Apple. Web Player Upgrades After last month’s major update to our embedded player , some of those upgrades are now live on RSS.com’s web player too! Listeners can now enjoy follow-along transcripts and interactive chapter markers right on your podcast’s public page.
