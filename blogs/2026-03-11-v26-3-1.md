New Feature 💎 Ad-Free Episodes for Apple Podcasts Subscribers If you are using PAID to monetize your show with programmatic ads, you now have another way to generate revenue: deliver ad-free episodes exclusively to your Apple Podcasts subscribers, all managed directly from your RSS.com Dashboard under Monetization → Apple Podcasts Subscriptions . Apple Podcasts is installed on over 2 billion active Apple devices. Subscribing takes one tap, payment is handled entirely by Apple, and the premium experience starts immediately with no friction. You publish one episode on RSS.com, PAID inserts ads for your public listeners, and Apple subscribers automatically get the ad-free version. You earn from both audiences at the same time, with no extra work. One episode. Two revenue streams. New Feature 🤖 AI disclosure AI is already part of how podcasts are made, from editing and transcription to writing episode notes, and in some cases even recording entire episodes. The question is not whether podcasters use it, but whether listeners can tell. We added a simple checkbox at the episode level that lets you disclose AI-generated content. The disclosure appears as a label on your podcast website and is embedded in your RSS feed, making it readable by any app or directory. With regulators and listeners increasingly demanding transparency, disclosing now keeps your podcast ahead of the curve. Not sure when to check the box? Visit shouldidisclose.ai to help you decide. Improvement 📱 PodLP Distribution We have added PodLP to the Distribution section in our Dashboard and as a Listen On button on your podcast website. PodLP is a podcast listening app with 10M+ installs built specifically for feature phones, the basic, low-cost mobile devices that are still the primary way millions of people in developing regions access the internet. Your show is now discoverable and accessible to new audiences that most podcast platforms never reach.
