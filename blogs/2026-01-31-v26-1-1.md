New Feature ⬇️ Episode Sorting Controls Our podcasters asked for better ways to sort their episodes, especially when managing a large back catalog. You can now sort episodes in your Dashboard by newest or oldest, making it easier to get to older episodes. New Feature 📊 Custom Date Range Selection in Analytics Analytics now supports custom date ranges. Whether you want to review last week or skip Mondays entirely and only look at Tuesday to Friday, you can pick exact dates and generate the report you need. Improvement 🍿 Do Even More With YouTube We improved our audio-to-video YouTube publishing feature in the Distribution tab, both for performance and user experience. It is now simpler to connect your channel and sync your playlist. Improvement 🗑️ You Can (Finally) Delete Episode Art You can now delete episode art. Previously, you could upload new episode art and replace the existing one, but you could not remove it entirely, which made “no episode art at all” a surprisingly hard request. Welcome to the long overdue Delete button.
