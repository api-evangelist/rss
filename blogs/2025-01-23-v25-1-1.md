Improvement Happy New Year! (Yes, we know it's almost the end of January, but it still counts, right?) Life is all about balancing big dreams and small wins. At RSS.com, while our team is cooking up some big projects (stay tuned, exciting news is coming!), we are also constantly refining and upgrading the little things. The first month of 2025 brings a bunch of subtle yet meaningful updates to make your experience better. Here's what we have been up to: Web Player with iOS Dynamic Island Support : Our web player now works beautifully with Apple's Dynamic Island on iPhones and iPads. What's Dynamic Island? Think of it as a fancy little butler at the top of your screen, ready to serve updates and controls. Now, your podcasts get the VIP treatment: you'll see cover art at a glance while playing in the background and even interact with the episode directly. A small but delightful upgrade iOS users will love! Podroll Upgrade : We made Podroll synchronization smoother. Not sure what a Podroll is? You're missing out! Go check it out and give your podcast some extra shine. Dashboard Fixes : No more runaway soundbite titles or overlapping chart labels on mobile. We tidied up those pesky glitches so everything looks crisp and clean. Country Display Standardization : Bye-bye to cryptic country codes like CH and AT in our analytics. From now on, you'll see the full names: Switzerland and Austria. Because who has time to learn ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 codes anyway? Audio Analysis Improvements : Each time you upload a new audio file, we now perform better integrity checks and metadata extraction. Your audio is in safe hands, and the process is even more robust. Lucky you, you must be young. Go ahead, look it up on TikTok or whatever you use for searches these days! Here's to a fantastic 2025, with plenty of updates (and surprises) on the way! 🚀
