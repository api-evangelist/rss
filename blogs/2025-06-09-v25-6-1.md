New Feature New Embedded Player Customization We’ve added new customization options to our embedded player, giving you more control over how your show looks and feels across the web. You can now choose to show or hide skip buttons, volume control, the share section, and the “About” area with your podcast description. Plus, users on paid plans can remove RSS.com branding for an even cleaner look. These settings are available at both the podcast and episode level, right from your Dashboard or public show page. Try it out and make the player truly your own!
