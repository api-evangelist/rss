New Feature 📍 Location Tag: Put Your Podcast on the Map (Literally) Over a year ago, we added support for the Podcasting 2.0 Location Tag . Since then, the standard has evolved with a major update, and so did we. You can now specify what place your podcast or episode is about , and where it was made . The new version integrates beautifully with OpenStreetMap, the open, community-driven project that powers maps around the world. It's a perfect match for podcasting: both are open, collaborative, and built by passionate people who love sharing knowledge. We now store not only the GPS coordinates but also the official OpenStreetMap ID . The location instantly appears on the podcast website we host for you, and our embedded player has also been updated to display it so your listeners can see exactly where your stories come from and what place they are about. And here's the exciting part : imagine what comes next when many of us start using this tag… Podcast apps and directories will soon let you explore shows recorded near you , episodes about hidden local gems in the places you're visiting , stories about landmarks in the spots you plan to explore , or even true crime series set in your new neighborhood 🕵️‍♂️ The map of podcasting just got a lot more interesting 🗺️ It's an easy win: go ahead and 📍 tag your show and episodes with a location! New Feature 🛠️ New Find My Feed Tool Ever noticed how the simplest things—like finding a podcast's RSS feed—can sometimes be trickier than they should be?
With our new Find My Feed tool, you can just enter a podcast’s title and get its RSS feed URL. We also gathered all our free tools (available to everyone, whether you host with RSS.com or not) into one easy place . Improvement 📣 New Distribution Destination: Curiocaster Building on our automatic submission engine , including Apple Podcasts and all major directories and listening apps, we have now added Curiocaster , giving you yet another way to share your podcast with the world. Improvement A few more improvements worth mentioning: 💸 Refinements to PAID : We gave PAID, our programmatic ads solution , a solid boost under the hood. From verification to payouts, the entire engine now runs more efficiently, sending 💰💰💰 every month to our podcasters around the world. 🖼️ High-Resolution Cover Art : Podcast and episode cover art now looks extra sharp on public pages and inside the dashboard—fully optimized for those fancy high-resolution devices some of you clearly splurged on 😎. 💳 Coupons Made Clear : For those of us who can't resist hunting for a coupon code before hitting "Buy", upgrading is now clearer: the full discount amount shows upfront, so you always know exactly what you're paying (and saving). 🌐 New Language Support : We added Tamil as a new language option for podcast RSS feeds. Vanakkam to all our Tamil-speaking creators and listeners 🎙️.
