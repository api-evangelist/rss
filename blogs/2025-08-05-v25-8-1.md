New Feature 🌒 Dark Mode Has Arrived Your eyes asked. We listened. Dark Mode is now available in your dashboard. Perfect for late-night or early-morning publishing, or just looking cool while managing your podcast with RSS.com. Dark Mode follows your system preferences by default, but you can always head to your account settings to flip the switch. New Feature 🥇 You Deserve a Badge Only 1 in 10 podcasters publish 8 episodes. Less than 2% make it to 50. If you have hit those milestones, you are now officially a podcasting legend, and we made you a badge to prove it. Wear it with pride. Improvement A Cleaner, Sharper Dashboard The top notification bar now stretches edge to edge, making room for the stuff that matters. Plus, we gave the navigation bar a refresh for a smoother, more consistent experience across desktop and mobile. 👀 In case you missed it Last month we added new customization options to our embedded player , one-click Apple Podcasts distribution, and also faster transcripts , now in VTT, too.
