New Feature 🛍️ More Monetization Opportunities for Everyone Pick sponsors. Get paid. No matter your audience size. We have teamed up with CodeADX so podcasters can connect with brands and share exclusive offers using custom promo codes. Mention your unique promo code in your episodes. Add it to your show notes for cross-promotion. Earn up to 20% commission every time a listener uses your code. 👉 Head to the Monetization Tab in your RSS.com Dashboard → CodeADX to get started. No minimum downloads needed. We are always looking for new ways to help you earn from your podcast, and this is another exciting addition to the mix. Alongside our recently launched 1-click “set it and forget it” programmatic ads solution PAID , Apple Podcasts Subscriptions , the Funding button, and Value 4 Value , you now have even more ways to make money. Improvement Update your PayPal Address You can now update the PayPal address used for PAID payouts directly from your RSS.com dashboard. No support tickets, no hassle. Just update it yourself and keep the earnings flowing. 💸 Improvement Better PAID Experience: Embed Player & Category Controls The embed player now works even more smoothly with podcasts using PAID monetization, automatically adjusting episode length to account for ad slots for a seamless listener experience. We have also refined how you manage IAB category exclusions in PAID. Excluding categories is simpler, and you can restore the default setup with just one click. Improvement ☕️ Brewing Analytics Faster Than Ever Not every improvement is something you can see, but some you can definitely feel. We have supercharged the way we brew your podcast analytics. Before: it was like waiting on a slow drip coffee. 
Now: it is like pressing a button and getting a perfect espresso shot. ⚡️ What is happening under the hood really matters: your analytics are now faster, stronger, and roasted to perfection by our engineering team. 🚀 Fix 🌙 Dark Mode… but Not Too Dark! We recently rolled out Dark Mode for the Dashboard to keep your eyes happy during those late-night publishing sessions. A few elements turned out a little too dark, though, making content hard to see. We adjusted the contrast so it stays dark, but you will not need night-vision goggles to read it. 😎
