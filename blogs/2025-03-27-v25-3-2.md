New Feature RSS.com is now IAB Certified Since launching new Podcast Analytics in September 2024 , RSS.com has been helping podcasters gain accurate and actionable insights by developing new tools that drive growth, engagement, and long-term success. Today we leveled up again: RSS.com Podcast Analytics were independently audited and are now officially compliant with the IAB Podcast Measurement Guidelines v2.2 . Why is this important? IAB certification gives podcasters like you the confidence that your audience and engagement metrics meet the highest industry standards, helping you grow your show, measure its success, and unlock even more opportunities for monetization and sponsorship through industry-backed credibility. It's official: your numbers have never looked better! 📈
