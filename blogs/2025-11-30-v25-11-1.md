Improvement 🔭 Longer Lookback for Analytics If you are on a Podcast Networks plan, your analytics just got a big upgrade. You can now view up to a full year of data. This makes it easier to spot trends, compare episodes over time, and do deeper performance reviews for all your shows. Improvement 🧭 New Navigation If you manage podcasts from your phone, this one is for you. We redesigned the main navigation so the podcast switcher now lives alongside your account actions in the top bar. The result: more usable screen space, fewer taps, and a cleaner experience on mobile and every other device size too. Improvement 🎥 Faster YouTube Publishing and a New "Listen On YouTube" Button We rebuilt our Audio-to-YouTube service from the ground up, and the results are clear. Converting your audio episodes into videos is now up to 4× faster than before, so your episodes reach YouTube in record time. We also refreshed the public podcast and episode pages. If your show is published on YouTube through our Audio-to-YouTube Conversion feature, you will now see a new Listen on YouTube button directly in the Listen On section of your podcast website.
