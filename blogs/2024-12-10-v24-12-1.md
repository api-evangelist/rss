New Feature Preview and Share Scheduled Episodes Promoting your podcast episodes just got easier! You can now access and copy the public URL of an episode even while it’s still in the Scheduled status. This feature is perfect for podcasters who release episodes at regular intervals and schedule them in advance, often alongside automated social media posts using tools like Hootsuite. With the new Share Preview option for scheduled episodes, you can generate a URL that links directly to the episode’s page before it goes live. This makes it easy to schedule social media promotions, coordinate campaigns, or share the link internally: all well in advance of publication!
