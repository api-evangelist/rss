New Feature Apple Podcasts Reviews Stay on top of audience feedback with our new Apple Podcasts Reviews feature, now available under the Analytics tab in our Dashboard! Easily access and track the latest Apple Podcasts reviews from around the world, helping you better understand your listeners, make informed content decisions, and engage with your audience more effectively. Improvement Episode Comparison Visualization You can now compare two episodes with different release dates more effectively. The starting point for comparison is now relative to both episodes, making it easier to analyze performance trends. Enhanced Analytics Export We have added the ability to export the full list of countries where your podcast is being listened to. This update allows for more granular insights and better audience segmentation. New Feature UI Refresh – A Sleek, Modern Update A new flat UI design has been rolled out across the Monetization, Distribution, and Settings sections of our Dashboard. This update marks the completion of our year-long transition to a more modern, accessible, and streamlined user experience. We are excited for you to explore these new improvements and look forward to hearing your feedback. Happy podcasting!
