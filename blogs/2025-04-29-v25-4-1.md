Improvement One RSS feed, endless possibilities RSS feeds remain the most powerful distribution method in podcasting, enabling your voice to reach listeners across countless apps and platforms. All from a single feed! Following the release of automatic Apple Podcasts submission last month, we have improved the Distribution page to showcase even more listening apps and directories featuring your podcast, including Pocket Casts, Boomplay, Castamatic, Fountain, Podcast Addict, Overcast, Podverse, TrueFans, and more, making it easier to see exactly where listeners can discover and enjoy your show. Improvement Bluesky sharing Remember when the bird was blue, before it became an X? Bluesky started as an internal project at Twitter to build a decentralized social media platform, and later became an independent company. As Bluesky continues to gain traction, especially within the podcasting community, we have made it easy for you to share your podcast and episodes directly to Bluesky from both your Dashboard and your podcast website, extending your social reach even further. Also, you can follow us on Bluesky . 👀 In case you missed it Earlier this year, we introduced the ability to track your global Apple Podcasts Reviews directly in your Dashboard, helping you stay connected with listeners and collect valuable feedback. Also, our Podcast Analytics recently obtained the official IAB Certification , providing you with reliable, industry-standard data to confidently grow your show and attract advertisers.
