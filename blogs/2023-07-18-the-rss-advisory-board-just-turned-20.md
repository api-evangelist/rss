"Tomorrow we will run faster, stretch out our arms farther." Today is the 20th birthday of the RSS Advisory Board, the group that publishes the RSS specification . It was formed on July 18, 2003, when the copyright of the specification was transferred to Harvard University, which immediately released it under a Creative Commons license and deferred all matters related to RSS to the new board.
